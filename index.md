# Privacy Policy for Pocket2FA

**Last updated:** April 20, 2026

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Pocket2FA ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

## 2. Information We Do Not Collect

Pocket2FA does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or user information of any kind.

Specifically, we do not collect:

- Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number, etc.)
- Device information or identifiers
- Location data
- Usage data or analytics
- Cookies or tracking technologies
- Any other user data

## 3. Local Data Storage

All data generated by the app (such as 2FA account tokens and settings) is stored **locally on your device** only. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any external servers, including ours.

## 4. No Third-Party Services

Pocket2FA does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or external APIs that could access your data.

## 5. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, security is maintained through your device's built-in security features (such as device encryption, screen locks, and biometric authentication). We recommend keeping your device's operating system and security features up to date.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Pocket2FA does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
